Indicator tubes " HIGITEST "

ISO 9001

" HIGITEST " indicator tubesare used to quickly determine the concentration of toxic gases and vapors in the air of the working environment. Definitions are performed very quickly, conveniently and cheaply. No special qualification is required from the personnel performing the determination. Therefore, indicator tubes are widely used in all branches of industry, agriculture, transport, etc. Some of the areas of application are the search for gaps in equipment, control of technological processes, determination of toxic substances in the air of the working and environmental environment in view of its hygienic assessment, proving the presence of toxic or fire-hazardous substances in closed rooms, determining the personal exposure of workers, determining gas components in liquids, etc. The " HIGITEST " indicator tubes are glass tubes filled with a powdery material - carrier, on which a suitable chemical reagent has been applied. The ends of the tube are soldered, which is why its contents are isolated from the environment. Before conducting the analysis, the ends of the tube are broken, it is placed in the opening of the special sample pump type " HIGITEST "" or "Dreger", through it the amount of air determined for each type of tube is sucked in. In the presence of the analyzed substance, the indicator reagent applied to the carrier in the tube changes its color, and the length of the colored column is directly dependent on the concentration of the determined substance The concentration is read directly on the scale applied to the outer surface of the indicator tube. All the data necessary to carry out the analysis reproducibly and obtain accurate results are contained in the detailed operating instructions that are inserted in each package of indicator tubes.
