Tenax Sorbent Tubes

Tenax® Sorbent Tubes, featuring both Tenax TA and Tenax GR variants, are acclaimed for their unparalleled efficiency in trapping medium to high boiling compounds in air samples. Tenax TA, a traditional porous polymer sorbent, boasts low background levels, making it exceptionally suitable for capturing low concentrations of target compounds. Its hydrophobic nature renders it ideal for applications such as EPA Method TO-17 or IP-1B, as well as various thermal desorption techniques. Expanding the capabilities of Tenax TA, Tenax GR represents a Tenax/graphite composite that broadens the sorbent's range to include lower boiling compounds. While retaining the ease of cleaning and low background characteristics of Tenax TA, Tenax GR further enhances the versatility of thermal desorption applications. Tenax Sorbent Tubes offer several advantages beyond their exceptional trapping efficiency. They are renowned for their robustness, ensuring reliable performance in diverse environmental and industrial settings. Additionally, their compatibility with thermal desorption instrumentation facilitates seamless integration into analytical workflows, enabling accurate quantification of target compounds. Moreover, Tenax Sorbent Tubes are trusted by researchers, environmental consultants, and regulatory agencies worldwide for their reliability, consistency, and ease of use. Whether employed in ambient air monitoring, emissions testing, or occupational exposure assessments, these tubes play a pivotal role in advancing air quality research and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards. In summary, Tenax Sorbent Tubes stand as indispensable tools for air sampling applications where the precise capture and analysis of volatile organic compounds are paramount. Their versatility, reliability, and proven performance make them a preferred choice among professionals seeking to safeguard human health and protect the environment.
